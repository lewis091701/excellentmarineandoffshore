Our Services

Premium Base Oils

SN500 Base Oil - Superior Quality Lubricant Base

Our SN500 base oil represents the pinnacle of refined petroleum products. This high-grade lubricant base stock is essential for manufacturing premium motor oils, industrial lubricants, and specialty oils. With its exceptional viscosity index and thermal stability, SN500 ensures optimal performance in demanding applications across marine, automotive, and industrial sectors.

Marine Storage & Logistics

World-Class Petroleum Storage & Distribution

Our state-of-the-art marine storage facilities feature massive tank farms capable of storing millions of barrels of petroleum products. Strategically located at major ports, these facilities ensure efficient loading, unloading, and distribution of crude oil, refined products, and specialty chemicals. With advanced safety systems and environmental protection measures, we guarantee secure and compliant operations.

Our Operations

SN500 Base Oil - Laboratory Grade

Our SN500 base oil undergoes rigorous quality testing in our certified laboratories. This premium lubricant base stock meets international standards for viscosity, purity, and performance. Used in manufacturing high-quality motor oils, hydraulic fluids, and industrial lubricants, SN500 represents excellence in petroleum refining technology.
